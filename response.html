"sales by month over the last 6 months"

Answer: Query executed successfully.

Generated SQL

SELECT DATE_FORMAT(o.order_date, '%Y-%m') AS month, SUM(oi.qty * oi.unit_price * (1 - oi.discount_pct/100)) AS total_sales FROM orders o JOIN order_items oi ON oi.order_id = o.id WHERE o.status <> 'CANCELLED' AND o.order_date >= DATE_SUB(CURDATE(), INTERVAL 6 MONTH) GROUP BY month ORDER BY month;
